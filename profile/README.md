## Overview

<a href="#">Live Home 3D Pro</a> enables anyone to design interior spaces with professional results. No architecture or design training required - the intuitive interface guides you through creating floor plans, furnishing rooms, and visualizing completed designs. See your ideas take shape in realistic 3D before committing to real-world changes.

The <a href="#">floor plan editor</a> establishes room layouts with precision. Draw walls by specifying dimensions exactly. Add doors and windows that create proper openings. Partition spaces into rooms that calculate area automatically. This 2D foundation supports the 3D visualization that brings designs to life.

<a href="#">Furniture placement</a> fills spaces with realistic objects. Browse an extensive catalog of furniture, appliances, and decor items. Drag items into rooms and position precisely. Resize objects to match real-world dimensions. The furniture library covers every room type and design style.

The <a href="#">material editor</a> customizes surfaces throughout your design. Apply wall colors and textures. Choose flooring materials from hardwood to carpet to tile. Select countertop materials for kitchens and baths. These material choices dramatically affect how spaces feel.

<a href="#">3D visualization</a> transforms floor plans into walkable environments. Navigate through your design at life scale. See how rooms feel before they exist physically. Check sight lines and proportions that flat drawings can't convey. This visualization validates designs experientially.

The <a href="#">lighting system</a> shows how spaces appear under different conditions. Natural light enters through windows based on orientation. Add artificial lighting from the fixture library. Adjust ambient levels for day and night appearance. Lighting dramatically affects design perception.

<a href="#">Camera views</a> capture design perspectives as images. Position cameras at compelling angles. Render high-resolution images for presentation. Create multiple views showing different aspects of designs. These rendered images communicate designs to others effectively.
